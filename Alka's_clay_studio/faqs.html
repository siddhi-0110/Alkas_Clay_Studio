Q: Are all the products at Alka's Clay Studio handmade?
A: Yes, every item in our collection is meticulously handcrafted by skilled artisans at Alka's Clay Studio.

Q: Can I customize a piece to my preferences?
A: While we offer a curated selection, we currently do not offer customization options. Each piece is unique and carries its own artistic value.

Q: How do I care for my pottery items?
A: Pottery is delicate. We recommend hand washing your pieces gently with mild soap. Avoid using abrasive materials that may scratch the surface.

Q: What materials are used in the pottery items?
A: Our pottery is primarily made from high-quality clay that is kiln-fired to perfection.

Q: Do you offer international shipping?
A: Yes, we offer international shipping. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary based on your location.

Q: Can I return or exchange a product if I'm not satisfied?
A: We strive for your satisfaction. If you receive a damaged item or are not satisfied with your purchase, please contact us within 7 days of delivery for assistance.

Q: How can I track my order?
A: Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to monitor the delivery status.

Q: Is your packaging eco-friendly?
A: Yes, we are committed to sustainability. Our packaging materials are chosen with the environment in mind.

Q: Do you offer wholesale or bulk purchasing options?
A: Yes, we do. Please reach out to us with your requirements, and we'll be happy to assist you.

Q: Can I visit your studio in person?
A: Currently, our studio is not open to the public. However, you can explore and purchase our collection through our online store.

Q: How do I contact your customer support?
A: You can reach our customer support team through the "Contact Us" section on our website or by emailing support@alkasclaystudio.com.
